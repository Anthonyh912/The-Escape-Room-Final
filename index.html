Welcome To The Escape Room

Welcome to the Escape Room a game dedicated to the point of view of a foster care child. Yearly children are placed in foster care systems under terrible living conditions, and are neglected. They are roughly impacted as the child may suffer from mal nutrition, drug abuse as well as mental and physical abuse. At the age of 18 the child is considered an adult and has absolutely no support and will most likely end up homeless, or if not in a shelter. Your job is to leave the system and enjoy your new life... Good Luck!

You must leave this place before it's too late. But how will you escape without getting caught? Do you just simply walk out the door like everyone else or will you climb out your bedroom window so no one will notice?
